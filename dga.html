Pixel Project

Why choose pixel as a medium for an artwork?

Using Pixel offers a unique mix of creativity and challenge — it's engaging, occasionally frustrating, and ultimately rewarding. Of course, it's also a requirement set by the lecturer.

The Process

It started as a simple idea to create a cute character that will be whimsical and satisfy my fascination with cute fluffy things. After searching the internet for a suitable mascot for this project, I stumbled across a furry friend, Spank. This then later inspired me to create the pixel character that has been submitted.

After choosing the reference, I then selected the software that would help me create my desired character, which turned out to be Piskel for its benefits and ease of use.

Side note: Piskel is an easy software that is free, doesn’t require downloads, and doesn’t demand previous knowledge of pixel characters or development.

  • Another thing that I would keep im mind is striving to create a sprite sheet in less fmames than I did.

Final outcome satisfaction

Overall, I a pretty satisfied with how my Spank turned out, just the right level of chubbiness, cutness and playfulness. I enjoy how everything came together at the end and made my 2D character have some personality and dimention.
